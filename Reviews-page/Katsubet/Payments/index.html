KatsuBet follows industry‑standard procedures for identity verification (KYC/AML). You may be required to submit a photo ID, proof of address and proof of payment method before making withdrawals—especially large ones or those involving significant sums.

The so‑called “closed‑loop” or “matching method” rule applies: you are generally required to withdraw via the same method you used to deposit, or a method linked to the deposit method, to satisfy anti‑fraud compliance. Failure to match may cause delays or partial payments.

Cryptocurrency Options

Deposit Minimums and Maximums

The minimum deposit amount at GG.Bet varies depending on the method and currency. These figures are indicative and may change depending on regional restrictions:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Minimum €5 / Maximum varies by card provider and region, often capped at €10,000 per transaction.

Withdrawal Minimums and Maximums

Processing Times

Processing times vary according to the chosen withdrawal method:

Deposits are generally faster than withdrawals, and crypto transactions are often the quickest option for both depositing and withdrawing funds.

Verification Requirements (KYC)

GG.Bet follows strict KYC (Know Your Customer) policies to prevent fraud and comply with regulations. Verification is often required before your first withdrawal, or when you request large withdrawals.

Ensuring your account is verified in advance prevents delays when requesting withdrawals, especially large ones. GG.Bet reserves the right to request additional documents at any time.

Best Esports Betting Sites: League of Legends, CS, Dota 2, Valorant and more !

Esports has gone from a niche hobby to a global phenomenon with millions of fans. With events like CS Blast Premier World Final, Dota 2’s The International, and League of Legends World Championship getting millions of viewers and prize pools in the millions, the betting world has taken notice.

Now fans of competitive gaming can engage with their favourite esports in a whole new way by betting on online bookies.

In this guide, we’ll look at the best esports betting sites that offer markets for CS, Dota 2, League of Legends, and Valorant.

Whether you’re a seasoned bettor or new to esports betting, we’ll help you navigate the top betting sites that are secure, reliable, and fun to bet on.

Why Bet on Esports?

The thrill of esports betting comes from the fast-paced action, unpredictability, and sheer excitement of watching pro gamers play.

Esports bookmakers offer a range of markets so you can bet on match winners, tournament champions, in-game achievements, and more.

Games like CS, Dota 2, and Valorant are popular because of their global tournaments, high level strategy, and fast-paced gameplay.

Live esports betting adds another layer of excitement by allowing fans to bet in real-time, making each round or match even more intense.

Whether you’re betting on a CS clutch, a League of Legends Baron steal, or a last minute push in Dota 2, the options are endless.

Best CS Betting Sites for Esports Enthusiasts

One of the most popular games in esports betting is Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS).

With events like the Blast Premier World Final and the PGL Major, CS is popular with both seasoned bettors and newbies.

When looking for the best gambling site for CS, you need to find platforms that offer competitive odds, multiple markets, and secure payment options.

  • Multiple options to bet: Bet on match winners, tournament champions, pistol rounds, first kills, and even specific player performances.

  • Bonuses for new players: Many CSGO betting sites offer bonuses and promotions, free bets, or deposit matches for new users.

  • Live CS betting: Live esports betting for ongoing matches so you can bet in real-time and adjust your strategy based on in-game events.

With the right CS site you can fully engage with competitive gaming and make every headshot and round win more exciting than ever.

Dota 2 Betting: Bet on the biggest MOBA tournaments

If you’re a fan of massive multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games, Dota 2 should be on your watchlist.

With some of the biggest prize pools in esports history including The International, Dota 2 betting is popular among gamers and bettors.

The top Dota 2 betting sites offer markets for match winners, total kills, hero picks, and game duration.

When betting on Dota 2:

  • Statistics: Dota 2 is a game of strategy and teamwork, and knowing player stats, team synergy, and previous performances can give you an edge when betting.

  • Markets: From who will take the first tower to who will get the most kills, Dota 2 offers a lot of betting options.

  • Tips: Many esports tips sites offer expert analysis and predictions to help you make informed bets, especially during big events like The International.

League of Legends (LoL) Betting

League of Legends (LoL) is another big player in the esports world.

With events like the World Championship, it consistently attracts millions of viewers and a big betting community.

Betting on LoL matches offers a lot of options, from simple bets like match winners to more specific bets like first blood or first dragon.

Here are some things to consider when betting on LoL:

  • Team and player knowledge: Knowing the top teams and players in the competitive scene is key to successful betting. Teams like T1, G2 Esports, and Fnatic have a long history of success.

  • Meta knowledge: The game’s meta (the most powerful champions and strategies) changes constantly and can greatly impact match outcomes. Stay up-to-date with patch notes and current team compositions.

  • Live betting: Just like CS, many betting sites offer live, in-play betting for LoL matches. You can bet during Baron or Elder Dragon fights, adding a layer of excitement to your betting experience.

Valorant Betting: The New Kid on the Block

Riot Games’ Valorant has quickly gained popularity thanks to its fast-paced gameplay and strategic depth.

Betting on Valorant tournaments like the Valorant Champions Tour is becoming more popular, with Valorant betting sites offering a lot of markets, from round winners to match winners to map outcomes.

Valorant bettors should look at:

  • Agents and abilities: Like CS, Valorant is a tactical shooter but the unique agent abilities add an extra layer of complexity. Knowing the agents’ strengths and how teams use them is key to successful betting.

  • Tournament coverage: The best Valorant betting sites cover all major events, offering a lot of markets and live betting options.

  • Esports tips: Valorant is still new to the betting scene so following esports tips and expert analysis can be very helpful for new bettors.

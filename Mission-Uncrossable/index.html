Mission Uncrossable is a remake of the super popular mobile game “Crossy Chicken” with millions of players.

The theme is helping a chicken cross roads and obstacles. But the challenge is to avoid cars that can kill the chicken at any time.

Players will face different challenges as they navigate through traffic and avoid cars which can take many forms of traffic patterns and obstacles.

Players can also submit their high scores or achievements. The game ends when a player hits a car, cash out or complete a certain number of lanes.

BC.GAME Weekly Sports Bonus – Up to $1,000 Every Week

BC.GAME continues to reward its most active sportsbook players with an ongoing Weekly Sports Bonus. This promotion is designed exclusively for high-tier VIP members and offers up to $1,000 every week, based on total sportsbook wagering.

The bonus is credited automatically, requires no manual activation, and runs every week until further notice. For players who regularly place sports bets, this promotion adds extra value without changing their usual betting routine.

What Is the BC.GAME Weekly Sports Bonus?

The Weekly Sports Bonus is a recurring promotion available to VIP level 22 and above on BC.GAME. Eligible players who place real-money bets on any sportsbook markets during the weekly period are automatically rewarded based on their total wagering volume.

Bonuses are distributed every Saturday, with higher wager amounts unlocking higher bonus tiers.

Who Can Participate?

To qualify for the Weekly Sports Bonus, players must meet the following conditions:

  • Hold VIP level 22 or higher on BC.GAME
  • Place real-money bets on sportsbook markets only
  • Maintain VIP 22+ status during the weekly period
  • Bet within the defined weekly wagering timeframe

Casino wagers do not count toward this promotion.

How the Weekly Bonus Works

The promotion is fully automated and easy to follow:

  1. Place sports bets on any available sportsbook markets
  2. Accumulate total wagering throughout the week
  3. Receive your bonus automatically every Saturday

There is no need to opt in or manually claim the bonus.

Weekly Wagering Period

All qualifying wagers must be placed within the official weekly timeframe:

  • Start: Saturday at 00:00 UTC
  • End: Friday at 23:59 UTC

Wager totals reset every Saturday at 00:00 UTC.

Weekly Bonus Payout Structure

Your bonus is determined by the total amount wagered on sportsbook markets during the week:

  • $500 or more wagered – $5 bonus
  • $5,000 or more wagered – $70 bonus
  • $10,000 or more wagered – $150 bonus
  • $50,000 or more wagered – $500 bonus
  • $250,000 or more wagered – $1,000 bonus

Players receive the highest bonus tier reached during the week.

Bonus Payment Details

Bonuses are credited automatically every Saturday morning (UTC). The payout method depends on the account type:

  • Crypto users receive the bonus in BCD
  • Fiat users receive the bonus in their account currency

This promotion is separate from and additional to existing VIP weekly rewards.

Important Terms and Conditions

Before participating, players should be aware of the following rules:

  • Only sportsbook wagering counts toward the bonus
  • All sports, leagues, and odds qualify
  • Weekly wagering amounts reset every Saturday
  • VIP level 22+ status must be maintained to remain eligible
  • BC.GAME may review betting activity to ensure fair play
  • The promotion may be modified or ended at any time

In case of missing or incorrect bonus crediting, players should contact BC.GAME VIP support for assistance.

Final Thoughts

The BC.GAME Weekly Sports Bonus is a strong long-term incentive for VIP players who focus on sports betting. With automatic payouts, flexible wagering options, and bonuses reaching up to $1,000 per week, it rewards consistent activity without unnecessary complexity.

For VIP level 22+ members, this promotion adds steady extra value to every week of sportsbook betting.

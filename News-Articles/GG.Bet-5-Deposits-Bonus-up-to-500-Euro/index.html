GG.Bet Deposit Quest – 5 Deposits up to €500

GG.Bet has launched the Deposit Quest, a fresh and flexible deposit bonus that rewards players based on their deposit activity. How the bonus works

The Deposit Quest process is simple but time-sensitive. Follow these steps to claim the cash bonus:

  • Activate the offer: Go to the Promotions section in your GG.Bet account and enable the Deposit Quest.
  • Make five deposits: You must make 5 deposits, each at least €5, within 24 hours after activation.

Example: Deposits = €20, €50, €10, €40, €30 → Average = €30 → You receive a €30 cash bonus.

Wagering and cashout rules

As with most casino promotions, Deposit Quest comes with clear wagering and cashout terms:

Important conditions

Make sure you understand these conditions before starting the quest:

  • Standard terms apply: This offer is subject to GG.Bet’s standard casino bonus terms and conditions.

Why this promo appeals to players

The Deposit Quest stands out because it scales with your deposits.

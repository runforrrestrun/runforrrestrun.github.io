Introducing Same Game Combos at Thunderpick: Elevate Your Esports Betting

Thunderpick is revolutionizing esports betting with its latest feature: Same Game Combos (SGC). Also known as "Same Game Parlay" or "One Game Parlay," this unique betting option allows players to combine multiple selections from the same esports match into a single, high-stakes bet. This groundbreaking feature lets you create personalized, high-payout wagers by linking various events from the same game.

Thunderpick’s SGC feature currently covers League of Legends but is soon expanding to CS2, Dota 2, VALORANT, and more, bringing even more excitement to your esports betting experience. With bigger odds, bigger payouts, and bigger thrills, SGC takes esports betting to the next level.

How Same Game Combos Work

A Same Game Combo involves multiple selections from a single match. You can combine different betting markets like:

  • Map winners
  • Total maps played
  • Dragon types
  • Correct match score

Since all selections must win for the bet to be successful, this type of bet carries more risk but offers significantly higher rewards. The odds of a Same Game Combo are always higher than betting on each outcome individually.

Correlated Outcomes: SGC allows for smart betting on related events. For instance, if you expect a dominant performance from a team, you might also wager on them drawing first blood or the match duration.

Flexibility and Strategy: Bettors can tailor their wagers to match their predictions for the game. Combining different selections into a single bet requires precision and strategy—if any selection fails, the bet is lost.

Placing a Same Game Combo at Thunderpick

  • Step 1: Find a match that offers SGC bets by scrolling through the list of esports matches or using the SGC filter.
  • Step 2: Open the match page and navigate to the SGC section to explore available bets.
  • Step 3: Pick your selections and add them to your bet slip. Some selections may not be combinable with others.
  • Step 4: Place your bet, enter the wager amount, and confirm. Your SGC bet will appear in the "My Bets" section.

Key Points to Remember

  • All selections must be correct for the SGC to win.
  • The payout is typically higher due to increased risk.
  • SGC bets are only available before the match starts.

If you encounter any issues, Thunderpick’s support team is ready to assist.

Same Game Combo FAQs

What is a Same Game Combo?

A Same Game Combo allows you to combine multiple selections from one match into a single bet for a higher potential payout.

How is it different from a Combo Bet?

The key difference is that a Combo Bet spans multiple games, while a Same Game Combo focuses on one match.

Which esports support SGC?

Currently, SGC is only available for League of Legends. However, Thunderpick will soon introduce SGC for CS2, Dota 2, and VALORANT.

Why can’t I add certain bets to my SGC?

Some selections are mutually exclusive, preventing them from being combined in an SGC.

Why do SGC odds differ from single or Combo bets?

SGC odds are dynamic and reflect the interrelated nature of the events within a single match, leading to higher, unique odds for each combination.

Can I combine bets across multiple maps?

Yes, you can create a Same Game Combo across maps within a single match.

Is SGC available for traditional sports?

Not yet, but with enough demand, Thunderpick may expand SGC to traditional sports betting in the future.

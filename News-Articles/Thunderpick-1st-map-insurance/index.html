Worlds 2024 Knockout Stage: Thunderpick’s First Map Insurance Promo

As the Worlds 2024 Knockout Stage kicks off, the competition is about to reach new heights. With only eight teams remaining, every match is a high-stakes battle. But Thunderpick is here to lighten the load with their First Map Insurance Promo, offering players a chance to get a little extra security on their bets.

What Is the First Map Insurance Promo?

Starting from October 17, 2024, Thunderpick is rolling out a unique 1st Map Insurance promotion for Worlds 2024 fans. If your team wins the first map but ultimately loses the match, you can claim a refund of up to €50 on your bet. It’s a fantastic way to keep your spirits high, even if the outcome doesn’t go your way!

How It Works:

  • Pick Your Team: Choose who you think will win from any of the Worlds 2024 Knockout Stage matches.
  • Make Your Bet: Place a single, pre-match bet with a minimum value of €10.
  • Claim Your Refund: If your chosen team wins the first map but loses the overall match, you’re eligible for a refund of up to €50.

Why You Shouldn’t Miss Out

This promotion gives you a second chance during these high-pressure Knockout Stage games, where every map can make or break a team's journey. Whether you're a seasoned League of Legends bettor or just looking to add excitement to your Worlds 2024 experience, this insurance is an excellent way to bet with more confidence.

Important Dates and Terms:

  • Promo Period: From October 17, 2024, until November 2, 2024 (UTC).
  • Bet Type: Pre-match single bets (on match winner market).
  • Refund Details: Only one bet per match qualifies for a potential refund, up to €50.
  • Eligibility: Players must have registered and deposited before October 15, 2024.
  • Additional Requirements: Any refunded amount must meet a 3x rollover requirement before withdrawal.

How to Participate:

  1. Sign in to your Thunderpick account.
  2. Place your bet on a Knockout Stage match.
  3. If your team takes the first map but doesn't secure the series win, you'll receive your refund within 72 hours!

Make sure to get your bets in for Worlds 2024 and take advantage of Thunderpick's 1st Map Insurance. It's the perfect way to stay engaged, even when your team’s journey gets tough.

Get Ready for the Worlds Knockout Stage with Thunderpick!

For those who love the thrill of esports betting, Thunderpick offers a unique way to enjoy the League of Legends World Championship without all the risk. With the First Map Insurance, you’ll have a little extra protection while watching your favorite teams compete.

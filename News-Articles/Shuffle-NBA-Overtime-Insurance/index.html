NBA Overtime Insurance: Get a Refund if Your Team Loses in OT

Basketball fans know that games in the NBA can change dramatically in the final seconds. One moment your team is ahead, and the next the match heads into overtime where anything can happen. To soften the blow of those close losses, Shuffle has introduced a special Overtime Insurance promotion for NBA games.

With this offer, players who place a pre-match bet on the winner of an NBA game can receive a refund if their team ends up losing in overtime. It’s a safety net for one of the most frustrating situations in sports betting — when your pick survives regulation but falls short in extra time.

The promotion is available until June 29, 2026, giving bettors plenty of opportunities throughout the NBA season to take advantage of the offer.

How the Overtime Insurance Promotion Works

The concept behind the promotion is simple. If you place a bet on an NBA team to win and the game goes into overtime where your team loses, Shuffle may refund your wager.

However, several conditions must be met for the refund to apply.

  • The bet must be placed before the game starts.
  • The wager must be in the Winner (Including Overtime) market.
  • Only single bets qualify.
  • The first bet per game is the only one eligible.
  • The maximum refund is $200.

If all these requirements are met and your team loses during overtime, the wager may be refunded up to the promotion limit.

Refund Details

Eligible refunds are not credited instantly after the match ends. According to the promotion rules, it may take up to 72 hours for the refund to appear in your account.

The credited value will be equivalent to up to $200 USD. Shuffle may issue the refund in cryptocurrency that matches the USD value.

This means the refund will appear as account credit rather than a direct cash payout.

Extra Rewards for Sports Bettors

In addition to the overtime protection, Shuffle also rewards sports bettors through its experience system.

Sports wagers placed with odds higher than 1.10 earn 3 XP for every $1 wagered. These experience points help players progress within the platform and unlock additional benefits.

Important Terms to Keep in Mind

Players must follow the platform’s rules to remain eligible for the promotion. For example, using multiple accounts is not allowed. If multi-account activity is detected, those accounts may lose eligibility for refunds and could be restricted from participating in future promotions.

Shuffle also reserves the right to modify the promotion rules or disqualify participants who violate the platform’s terms of service.

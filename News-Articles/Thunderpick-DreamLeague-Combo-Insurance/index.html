Back Up Your DreamLeague 24 Bets with Thunderpick’s Combo Bet Insurance!

DreamLeague Season 24 Group Stage 2 is here, and Thunderpick has launched a special Combo Bet Insurance to make your bets even more thrilling. From November 1 to November 7, 2024, you can place multi-leg bets with the peace of mind that if you miss just one leg, Thunderpick has your back! Here’s how it works:

Promo Period:

November 1 – November 7, 2024 (UTC)

What’s Combo Bet Insurance?

Thunderpick’s Combo Bet Insurance allows you to place a multi-leg bet with a minimum of five selections. If all but one of your legs win, you can claim a refund of up to €50! Just make sure one of your selections is on a DreamLeague Season 24 Group Stage 2 match.

How to Qualify:

  • Place a Combo Bet: Bet with at least five legs, each with odds of 1.4 or higher.
  • Include a DreamLeague Match: At least one selection must be on a DreamLeague Group Stage 2 game.
  • Meet the Requirements: If only one of your legs loses, you’ll be eligible for a refund of up to €50!

Key Terms:

  • Only pre-match bets are eligible.
  • Bets must be placed with real funds—no cash-out options or voided events are allowed.
  • The promo applies once per day, with the first qualifying bet counted for the refund.
  • A 1x rollover is required on refunded amounts, credited within 72 hours after bet settlement.

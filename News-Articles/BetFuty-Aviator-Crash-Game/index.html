What Is Aviator?

Aviator is a popular crash game featuring an airplane theme. The game is simple yet captivating: as the round begins, an airplane takes off and a progress bar tracks its ascent. The challenge lies in deciding the perfect moment to cash out before the plane crashes. The longer you wait, the higher the potential multiplier and winnings, but the risk of the plane crashing increases.

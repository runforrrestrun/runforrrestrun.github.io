Thunderpick Skins Giveaway: Bet On TWC 2024 To Win CS2 Skins Worth Up To €5,000

Get Ready for the Thunderpick World Championship!

Thunderpick is excited to announce the Thunderpick Skins Giveaway in celebration of the Thunderpick World Championship (TWC) 2024! From April 1 to October 24, 2024, participants have the chance to win fantastic CS2 skins, with a total prize pool of skins worth up to €5,000!

How It Works

During each of the six qualifiers, players can enter to win a CS2 skin valued at €1,000. Plus, for those looking for a bigger prize, there’s a chance to win an exclusive skin worth up to €5,000 during the Main Event! Here’s how you can get involved:

  1. Bet at least €75 during each of the qualifiers to qualify for the skin giveaways.
  2. To be eligible for the final skin worth €5,000, ensure you place bets of at least €75 on all six qualifiers as well as the Main Event.

Promotion Schedule

The following qualifiers and the main event will be held as part of the TWC 2024:

  • Europe Qualifier #1: April 1 – April 22
  • South America Qualifier #1: May 11 – May 16
  • North America Qualifier #1: May 19 – May 24
  • North America Qualifier #2: June 4 – June 9
  • South America Qualifier #2: July 17 – July 22
  • Europe Qualifier #2: August 26 – September 7
  • Main Event: October 21 – November 3

How to Enter

WHAT: A total of seven CS2 skins are up for grabs: one worth up to €1,000 during each qualifier and one worth up to €5,000 during the Main Event.

WHEN: The giveaway runs from April 1 to October 24, 2024.

HOW: Place a bet of at least €75 during each qualifier for a chance to win, and make sure to place bets on the Main Event for the chance at the grand prize!

Terms and Conditions

  • Promotion Period: April 1, 2024 – November 3, 2024
  • Qualifying bets must be made during each of the stages mentioned above.
  • Only single (pre-match and live) bets using real money will count towards the promotion.
  • Cash-out or voided events are not eligible for this promotion.
  • Winners will be randomly selected and contacted via email; they must provide their full name, address, and phone number to claim the prize.
  • If the winner does not respond within a week, a new winner will be chosen.
  • Thunderpick reserves the right to modify the promotion's terms and conditions at any time.
